Content preferences

This page manages the content preference for the Apple TV that displayed this code. LoungeFeed keeps sensitive content masked by default.

Adult content

Some connected sources may label posts as adult or sensitive. Enabling this preference can allow that Apple TV to offer an additional local screen-protection step.

Allow the Apple TV to offer local accessThe Apple TV remains masked until its local protection is completed.

A valid Pro membership is required for this preference to take effect. Without it, enabling this switch does not reveal content.

This code is no longer active

Return to LoungeFeed on Apple TV and select Content preferences again to display a new code.

Privacy Policy · LoungeFeed stores only this Apple TV’s random preference identifier and the selected setting. It does not receive your feed content, media, social credentials, or Apple Account information.

Before you continue
